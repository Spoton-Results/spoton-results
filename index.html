FAQ

Answers to your
biggest questions

How much can I realistically expect to save?
+
Most businesses we work with save between 0.5% and 1.5% on their effective processing rate. For a business doing $50,000/month, that's $300–$750 per month, or $3,600–$9,000 per year. For higher-volume businesses, savings of $1,500–$3,000+/month are common. Use our savings calculator for your estimate, or book a free consultation for your exact numbers.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
+
No. We only work with processors that offer month-to-month agreements. We firmly believe you should never be penalized for wanting better service. This keeps us accountable and keeps us working hard to earn your business every single month.
How long does the switch take?
+
Most businesses are fully up and running with new rates within 24–48 hours. We coordinate everything — equipment ordering, software setup, and staff training — so there's zero downtime for your business. We time it so you're never without a way to accept payments.
What if I'm already locked into a contract?
+
We'll review your contract terms carefully. In many cases, the monthly savings from switching outweigh the exit fees within just 1–2 months. If it doesn't make financial sense to switch yet, we'll tell you honestly and help you plan the right time to move. We never pressure you into a decision that isn't in your best interest.
How does SpotOn Results make money?
+
We receive a referral fee from the payment processors we place business with — this never comes from your pocket and never affects your rates. Our model only works long-term if you're genuinely saving money and staying with us, so finding you the best possible rate is always our top priority.
Do you only work with businesses in Utah?
+
We're based in Utah and have deep relationships with hundreds of local businesses, but we serve businesses nationwide. Our entire process — statement analysis, setup, training, and ongoing support — can be handled completely remotely. Wherever you are, we can help.
